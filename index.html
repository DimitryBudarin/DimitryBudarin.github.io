Sock Analysis: Universe Selection

I start my search for "The ONE" portfolio right at the source. Analysing the fundamental data of 4396 listed US companies. The aim is to reduce this number to around 100 (data obtained from quarterly reports from Yahoo Finance):

- Removing any crazy outliers

- Filtering companies with high PE, Price to Book, PEG Ratios, and low Quick Ratios

- 10K Sentiment analysed using NLP (using vader dictionary)
