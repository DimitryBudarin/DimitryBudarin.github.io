Sock Analysis: Universe Selection

I start my search for "The ONE" portfolio right at the source. Analysing the fundamental data of 4396 listed US companies. The aim is to reduce this number to around 100 (data obtained from quarterly reports from Yahoo Finance):

- Removing any crazy outliers

- Filtering companies with high PE, Price to Book, PEG Ratios, and low Quick Ratios

Stock Sentiment Analysis with NLP and ChatGPT

- 10K Sentiment analysed using NLP (using vader dictionary)

Portfolio Optimisation and Factor Analysis

Here I perform portfolio optimisation by maximising Sharpe Ratio and comparing it to equally weighted portfolio. I construct efficient frontier and also analyse the portfolio based on Fama French Factor model: Value, Size, Aggressiveness

Performance, Sharpe Ratio, Drawdown, ES are calculated and compared against the market benchmark

Alternative Data: Maritime Bottleneck Identification and Economic Activity

This was my Master's thesis where I used AIS ship location data to estimate the effects that COVID pandemic had on waiting/unloading times of ships around the ports in the US and how this had an impact on economic activity. Project included:

- Obtaining and Cleaning over 1TB of shipping data

- Identifying and isolating relevant ships which were waiting to unload only

- Using Regression and Machine Learning methods to estimate economic impact
